Privacy Policy for PrairieTest Auto-Reserve

Last updated: March 3, 2026

PrairieTest Auto-Reserve does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data Storage
All user preferences (refresh intervals, preferred times and days, exam type 
filters, keyword filters, and course settings) are stored locally on your 
device using Chrome's built-in storage API. This data never leaves your device 
and is not accessible to the extension developer or any third party.

Permissions
The extension requires access to us.prairietest.com and ca.prairietest.com 
solely to monitor exam slot availability and perform reservations on your 
behalf. No data from these pages is collected or transmitted elsewhere.

Third Parties
This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising.

Changes
If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.

Contact
For questions, open an issue at the extension's GitHub repository.
